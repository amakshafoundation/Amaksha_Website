Education for Everyone

We believe that no child should be left behind due to financial limitations.

About Us

Amaksha Foundation is a non-profit initiative dedicated to ensuring quality education reaches underprivileged children across India. We provide scholarships, school supplies, and mentorship to support their learning journey.

Our Mission

To educate and empower students from financially weaker backgrounds by providing them access to resources, support, and opportunities to thrive academically and personally.
